# Ghost Rush

**Ghost Rush** is a combat-focused game made in python with pygame. Players fight ghosts and try to survive as long as possible. This game includes all necassary features from the competition rules.

---

## How to Play

Use arrow keys to move around the screen.
You can see your lives, health, and score at the top of the screen.
Survive waves of ghosts coming to attack you and defeat them.
Ghosts with more blood marks are stronger, faster, and use better attacks.
Attack Keys are A, S, and D for thrust, slash up, and slash down.
F and G are special attacks and have longer cooldowns.
Complete all three levels for victory!
